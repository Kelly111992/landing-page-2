# H2PRO — Design System (video)

Brand: H2PRO Protein Water. 1er Protein Water desarrollado y fabricado en México.

## Canvas
- Dark. 1080×1080 (square — embeds in an aspect-square slot on the landing page).
- Background: `#0a0e12` (ink). Soft panel: `#101820`.

## Color
- Foreground / text: `#f0ede8` (paper)
- Accent (one hue only): `#0086d6` (h2pro blue)
  - deep: `#00609a`
  - glow: `#4ab4f0`
- Tint neutrals cool toward the blue. No dead gray, no pure #000/#fff.

## Type
- Display / numbers / UI: **Manrope**, weight 800 (display), 600 (labels/eyebrow)
- Editorial accent words: **Instrument Serif**, weight 400, **upright (no italic)** — the brand dropped italics
- Eyebrow/labels: uppercase, letter-spacing ~0.22em

## Voice
Premium, honest, minimal. "Sin azúcar, sin lactosa, sin gomas. Etiqueta limpia."

## Don'ts
- No italics. No gradient text. No neon. No cyan — the blue is `#0086d6`, not `#00e5ff`.
